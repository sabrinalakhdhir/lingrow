Newcomers in Edmonton
Libraries

Libraries are free and accessible to everyone. To access services, you need to get a free library card from the library. With this free library card, you can borrow books, movies and music in 30 languages. You have access to a computer with internet, e-mail, printers and fax machines. You have free access to the Canadian Citizenship Practice Exams. You have access to family language kits in different languages to learn English (CD and book set, a picture dictionary and picture books). You have access to dual-language books (in two languages) for many different languages. You have access to resources for English Language tests. You have access to free online classes for babies and toddlers.
